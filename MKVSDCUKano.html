Kano

Bio

An undisciplined but dangerous thug, Kano is a killer for hire. His allegiance to the Black Dragon clan has been profitable, though dangerous - exactly the way he likes it. Kano joined Shao Kahn's ranks during the invasion of Earthrealm, forsaking his own realm for personal gain. For his crimes, he is constantly hunted by the Special Forces agent Sonya Blade. Though he enjoys the chase, he knows that one day he will finish her in Mortal Kombat. That day might soon be at hand.
